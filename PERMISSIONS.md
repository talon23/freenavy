# Free Navy permissions

## Base ranks

| Rank group | Ranks | Default authority |
|---|---|---|
| Owner | President | Complete portal authority and protected restoration controls |
| Executive | Vice President | Full operational administration except presidential-only actions |
| Senior Command | General, Admiral | Officer editing, operations, training, recruitment review and imports |
| Command | Vice Admiral, Rear Admiral, Brigadier General | Officer editing, operations, training, recruitment review and imports |
| Officer | Officer | Edit LIVE game data, approve submissions, manage operations and review applicants |
| Member | Enlisted | View, confirm, flag, submit and participate |

## Concurrent appointments

| Appointment | Adds |
|---|---|
| Admin | Website content, members, applications, imports, features, backgrounds, knowledge and backups |
| Quartermaster | Warehouse, reservations, equipment loans and warehouse approvals |
| Treasurer | Treasury, points, auction settlements and financial approvals |

Appointments do not replace rank. A member may hold several appointments.

## Protected capabilities

These cannot be granted by ordinary permission overrides:

- Assign command ranks
- Assign concurrent appointments
- Change the permission matrix
- Manage Identity-level protected controls
- Restore a complete backup

Ranks and appointments may be assigned only by the President or Vice President. Full backup restoration remains President-only.

## Member-facing data

- Enlisted members may view imported LIVE records.
- Enlisted members may flag a record and provide an explanation, suggested correction and evidence.
- Enlisted members may submit new locations, mining areas, salvage areas and coordinates for review.
- Officers and higher may edit imported data and resolve flags.
- Officer corrections are recorded in history and protected from silent source overwrite.

## Probation

Probationary members cannot access sensitive intelligence, auctions, treasury, high-value warehouse controls or approval queues. Probation ends automatically on the configured date or may be ended manually by authorised command.

## Dual approvals

A second authorised person is required when an action exceeds the configured warehouse or points threshold. The requester cannot approve their own request. The second approver must have the capability named on the request.
